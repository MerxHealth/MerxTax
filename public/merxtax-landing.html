The situation

MTD is mandatory. The tools aren't ready for you.

Sage and Xero were built for accountants. Not for a Brazilian self-employed cleaner in Southall, or a Portuguese landlord in Slough.

The problem today
❌All compliance tools are English-only — creating a systemic barrier for 1M+ diaspora taxpayers
❌Existing platforms are designed for accountants — confusing, expensive, and overbuilt for individuals
❌HMRC's quarterly deadline system penalises the uninformed with automatic penalty points
❌No WhatsApp-native experience — the primary communication channel for most diaspora communities
❌No AI to explain what's allowable, what's due, or what to do next — in plain language

How it works

Four steps to full compliance

From first login to submitted return — the entire MTD journey, simplified and guided in your language.

1
Connect to HMRC
Sign up, connect your HMRC account securely via OAuth 2.0. MerxTax is an authorised HMRC software provider.
2
Log income & expenses
Add income records, snap expense receipts, or connect your bank. LUMEN AI categorises everything automatically.
3
Review with VIGIL
Check your compliance score, review the quarter's figures, and let LUMEN AI flag anything that needs attention.
4
Submit to HMRC
One tap to submit via QUARTUS. Receive your HMRC confirmation, and VIGIL resets for the next quarter.
